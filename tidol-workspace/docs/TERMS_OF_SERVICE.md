## 2. Description of Service

Tidol is a **music search aggregator** that helps users discover music across multiple platforms. Tidol does NOT host, store, or distribute copyrighted audio content. Instead, Tidol:

- **Searches** for music metadata across official platform APIs (YouTube Data API, Spotify Web API, SoundCloud API, and Internet Archive).
- **Displays** results with embedded players from the original platforms.
- **Redirects** users to official platform pages for full playback.
- **Streams directly** only from Internet Archive, where content is licensed under Creative Commons or is in the public domain.
