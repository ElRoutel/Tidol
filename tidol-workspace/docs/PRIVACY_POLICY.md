# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** June 29, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

### Account Information
- Email address (for authentication)
- Username
- Hashed password (we never store plain-text passwords)

### Usage Data
- Search queries (for improving search relevance)
- Listening history (for personalized recommendations)
- Playlist data (user-created playlists)
- Like/favorite actions

### Technical Data
- IP address (for rate limiting and security)
- Browser user agent (for compatibility)

## 2. Information We Do NOT Collect

- **Audio files:** Tidol does not download, store, or cache any copyrighted audio content.
- **Payment information:** Tidol is a free service.
- **Location data:** We do not track precise geolocation.
- **Third-party platform credentials:** We never ask for your YouTube, Spotify, or SoundCloud passwords.

## 3. How We Use Information

- Provide personalized music recommendations based on listening history
- Improve search relevance
- Maintain service security and prevent abuse
- Generate anonymized usage statistics

## 4. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, rent, or share personal information with third parties, except:

- When required by law or legal process
- To protect the rights and safety of users and the service
- Anonymized, aggregated statistics (which cannot identify individuals)

## 5. Third-Party Services

Tidol uses embeds from YouTube, Spotify, and SoundCloud. When these embeds load, those platforms may set their own cookies and collect data according to their own privacy policies:

- [YouTube Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)
- [Spotify Privacy Policy](https://www.spotify.com/legal/privacy-policy/)
- [SoundCloud Privacy Policy](https://soundcloud.com/pages/privacy)

## 6. Data Retention

- Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
- Listening history may be deleted by the user at any time.
- Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.

## 7. Security

We use industry-standard security measures including:
- Password hashing with Argon2
- JWT-based authentication with secure token handling
- HTTPS encryption for all communications
- Rate limiting to prevent abuse

## 8. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your playlist and listening history data
- Opt out of personalized recommendations

## 9. Children's Privacy

Tidol is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

## 10. Changes

We may update this policy periodically. We will notify users of significant changes via email or in-app notification.
